About Us

Bubble Under is a group of family divers that formed in 1995 and we are happy to share our found passion with new and experienced divers alike.
My name is John Doe and I and the CEO and founder of Bubble Under. South Lake Tahoe is a beautiful land mark and underneath its waters is even more beautiful to the naked eye. Our team organizes weekly
meets with small groups on the weekends to ensure lower costs and that everyone has a diving buddy.

Although we're based in South Lake Tahoe, we also go to monthly meetups to other divers across the West Coast of the United States. The ocean reefs are absolutely beautiful.

When we're not diving, we often meet up in a bar and share stories.(any excuse,eh?)

Or as our man Bob Dobalina would put it:

"Happiness is a dip in the ocean followed by a pint or two of Old Speckled Hen. You can quote me on that!

"Join us for a weekend away at some of our favorite dive spots and you'll soon be making new friends.

"Anyway, about time I got on with some proper work!"
